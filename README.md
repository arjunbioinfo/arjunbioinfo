---

Postdoctoral researcher at **Karolinska Institutet** (Dept. of Oncology-Pathology), working at the
intersection of **chromatin biology, cancer genomics, and machine learning**. 7+ years turning
large-scale multi-omics data — from bulk to single-cell and spatial — into reproducible pipelines
and biological insight. PhD in Computational Systems Biology (University of Hyderabad, 2024).

### 🔬 Research focus

- **Genomics & single-cell multi-omics** — bulk/scRNA-seq, scATAC-seq, multiome, 10x Visium spatial
- **Cancer epigenomics** — ChIP-seq, ATAC-seq, CUT&RUN, GRO-seq; 3D genome & CTCF regulatory loops
- **Statistical ML for biology** — dimensionality reduction, latent-variable models, GRN inference
- **Reproducible pipelines** — Nextflow & Snakemake on Linux HPC
